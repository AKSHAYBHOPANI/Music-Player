What Is Bhangra Music?

Bhangra music is a folk music and dance form originating from Punjab, India. It is characterized by its energetic beats, lively rhythms, and celebratory nature. Traditionally associated with harvest festivals, Bhangra has evolved into a popular music genre enjoyed worldwide.

Bhangra music combines traditional Punjabi instruments with modern sounds, creating a unique blend that appeals to both traditional and contemporary audiences. The music is known for its ability to create a festive atmosphere and bring people together.
